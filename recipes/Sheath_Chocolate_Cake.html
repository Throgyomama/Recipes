Sheath Chocolate Cake

Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup Crisco
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 4 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Add flour, sugar, butter, crisco, and cocoa to a bowl
  2. Boil 1 cup of water and pour it into the bowl
  3. Turn oven to Bake and 400°F
  4. Add buttermilk, baking soda, vanilla, cinnamon, and eggs to the bowl mix together
  5. Pour mixture into a greased 9x13 pan
  6. Place pan in the oven for 20-25 minutes

Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 4 teaspoons cocoa
  • 6 tablespoons water
  • box of powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Begin 5 minutes before cake is finished
  2. Add butter, cocoa,and water into a pot
  3. Bring mixture to a boil then remove from heat
  4. Mix in powdered sugar
