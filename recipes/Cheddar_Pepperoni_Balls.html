Cheddar Pepperoni Balls

Ingredients

  • 8oz cheddar cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup margarine/butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons chopped pepperoni
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano

Steps

  1. Set oven to Bake at 425°F
  2. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix together
  3. Shape into equally sized balls
  4. Place on a sheet tray and place in oven for 15 minutes
