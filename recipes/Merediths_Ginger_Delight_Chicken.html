Meredith's Ginger Delight Chicken

Ingredients

  • 6-8 pieces of chicken (chicken breast and thighs)
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2-3/4 cup fruit juice
  • 2-3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons tamari or soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic or 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon fresh gingerroot or 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

Steps

  1. Shake chicken in bowl with flour
  2. Heat oil in Frying pan
  3. Add chicken and brown lightly
  4. In a bowl mix together juice, honey, soy sauce, ginger, and garlic
  5. Set oven to Bake at 375°F
  6. Remove chicken from Frying pan and place on a baking tray
  7. Pour your mixture over the chicken
  8. Place chicken in oven to bake for 35-40 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and serve with rice
