Noodles or Pasta dough

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil if making pasta dough
  • flour

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, water, and salt (as well as olive oil if making pasta)
  2. Add flour to make a dough until it is no longer sticky
  3. Knead until the dough won't take anymore flour then let it rest for 15 minutes
  4. Roll out dough until it is very thin
  5. Let dry before rolling up to cut -make sure there is enough flour under and on top of dough while drying
