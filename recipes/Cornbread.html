Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400°F
  2. Grease baking pan
  3. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl
  4. Stir in milk, oil, and egg until dry ingredients are moist
  5. Pour cornbread dough into the greased baking pan and bake for 20-25 minutes
  6. May use paper-lined muffin pans instead and bake for 15-20 minutes
