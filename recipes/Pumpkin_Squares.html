Pumpkin Squares

Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup pumpkin pur√©e
  • 1 cup oil
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Set oven to Bake at 350°F
  2. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl
  3. Pour into a greased 9x13 pan
  4. Bake for 20-30 minutes until tooth pick inserted comes back clean
