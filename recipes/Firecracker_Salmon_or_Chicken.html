Firecracker Salmon or Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons buffalo sauce (or favorite hot sauce)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1-2 teaspoons sriracha (adjust for heat)
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika (garnish or in marinade)
  • 1 pinch crushed red chili flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives
  • 4 skin removed salmon fillets or chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Add together in a bowl the buffalo sauce, soy sauce, olive oil, garlic, sriracha, brown sugar, ginger, and red chili flakes into a marinade (optional paprika
  2. Mix together into a marinade
  3. Coat the salmon or chicken in the marinade
  4. For best results refrigerate for 10 minutes but it can go straight into the oven
  5. Set the oven to Bake at 370°F for Salmon or 375°F for Chicken
  6. Bake Salmon for 12-15 minutes and Chicken for 20-30 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and serve with chopped chives garnish and a dash of paprika
