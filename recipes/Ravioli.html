Ravioli

Ingredients

  • 1 pasta dough
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl
  2. Combine the eggs with the ricotta, parmesan, salt, and pepper
  3. Beat them together thoroughly
  4. Prepare pasta dough
  5. Roll out part of pasta dough very thinly
  6. Make 2 inch squares of the dough placing mounds of the filling on each square
  7. Moisten with water then place thinly rolled dough on top and seal
  8. Continue sealing Ravioli noodles until finished
  9. Cook Ravioli in boiling water until they float to the surface
  10. Serve with your favorite tomato sauce and parmesan cheese
