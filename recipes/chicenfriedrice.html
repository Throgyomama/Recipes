Sriracha Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 jalapeños
  • 1 packet sriracha mix
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in instant pot and add packet of sriracha mix then pour water over and cook
  2. Dice jalapeños place to the side
  3. Remove chicken from instant pot and dice
  4. Add rice to the instant pot in with the chicken broth
  5. Add water until rice is submerged and mix rice in the water
  6. Cook Rice
  7. Place frying pan on stove at medium heat and scramble eggs
