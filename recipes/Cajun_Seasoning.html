Cajun Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Salt
  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon clove
  • 1 teaspoon allspice
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

Steps

  1. Place ingredients in a bowl
  2. Combine the ingredients
