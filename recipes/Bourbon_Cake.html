Bourbon Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound oleo
  • 1/2 pound butter
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 teaspoons almond extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup whiskey
  • 1 cup chopped nuts
  • 8 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix oleo, butter,2 cups sugar, vanilla, and almond extract
  2. Add Egg yolks one at a time to the mix keep the egg whites in a seperate bowl
  3. Slowly add flour, whiskey, and nuts
  4. Beat the egg whites until frothy then add 1 cup of sugar while beating
  5. Fold the two mixtures together
  6. Grease a pan and pour in the mix
  7. Set oven to Bake 350°F for 1 1/2 hours
